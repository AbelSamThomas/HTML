VIT Bhopal Gaming Department

Why Gaming?

By learning how to make games, your knowledge will be extended far beyond what you expected. You will learn many programming languages such as C++, C#, HTML5, and JavaScript. You will also be able to learn Android (if you are into mobile games), iOS, and many more.

Game dev at VIT BHOPAL

Game Development at VIT Bhopal is a fresh course that is aimed towards targetting the fastly growing field of gaming .The Gaming Department at VIT Bhopal has conducted 25+ events, 12 Master classes, 23 projects demonstrated in public and also has signed three MoUs with Unity Asia Pacific for Gaming, UI Path (USA) for RPA/Coding, Roach Interactive for Game Design. Also, group projects help students decide, discuss and implement their ideas to design games.
